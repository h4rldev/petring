How do i join?

  • Join us on Discord
  • Enter the #bot channel of the Jess's Museum discord server
  • Write the command /submit_user in the chat and then fill out the form that appears
  • Once the bot confirms your submittion is successful wait for a moderator or Jess herself to accept you into the webring
  • Then you'll show up here in this little ring :3

What is PetAds?

PetAds is a service specifically for petring members, allowing you to submit an image of your choosing and have it appear on a widget you can embed in your website, anyone can embed it, advertising your website to the greater internet :3
